# Paper Classification

## 1. Older adults: core qualitative studies

- `older-digital-technology-experiences` | *Older adults' digital technology experiences: a qualitative study* | 《老年人的数字技术使用体验：一项定性研究》
  Method: qualitative interviews
  Scenario: everyday digital life
  Friction focus: usability, learning cost, family support
- `older-web-based-services` | *Barriers to and Facilitators of Older People's Engagement With Web-Based Services: Qualitative Study of Adults Aged >75 Years* | 《老年人参与网络服务的障碍与促进因素：针对75岁以上人群的定性研究》
  Method: qualitative interviews
  Scenario: web-based public and daily services
  Friction focus: service access, support, trust
- `older-tablet-focus-group` | *Older Adults Perceptions of Technology and Barriers to Interacting with Tablet Computers: A Focus Group Study* | 《老年人对技术的看法以及与平板电脑交互的障碍：一项焦点小组研究》
  Method: focus groups
  Scenario: device adoption
  Friction focus: usability, anxiety, learning burden
- `older-digital-health-services-preferences` | *Older adults' preferences in the utilization of digital health and social services: a qualitative analysis of responses to open-ended questions* | 《老年人对数字健康与社会服务使用的偏好：对开放式问答的一项定性分析》
  Method: open-ended response analysis
  Scenario: digital health and social services
  Friction focus: accessibility, service design, support
- `older-health-purpose-qualitative` | *Older adults' experiences and perceptions of using digital technology for health purposes: A qualitative study* | 《老年人出于健康目的使用数字技术的经验与感知：一项定性研究》
  Method: qualitative interviews
  Scenario: digital health
  Friction focus: health-tech barriers and lived experience
- `older-modes-relating-icts` | *Modes of relating to the new ICTs among older internet users: a qualitative approach* | 《老年网民与新型信息通信技术的关系模式：一项定性研究》
  Method: qualitative interviews
  Scenario: older internet users
  Friction focus: heterogeneity of engagement

## 2. Older adults: core quantitative studies

- `older-self-perception-public-services` | *Older adults' self-perception, technology anxiety, and intention to use digital public services* | 《老年人的自我感知、技术焦虑与使用数字公共服务的意愿》
  Method: survey + SEM
  Scenario: digital public services
  Friction focus: psychological friction, technology anxiety
- `older-migrants-internet-barriers` | *Health and self-perceived barriers to internet use among older migrants: a population-based study* | 《老年移民的健康状况与自我感知的互联网使用障碍：一项基于人群的研究》
  Method: population survey
  Scenario: internet use
  Friction focus: cost, complexity, health-related barriers
- `older-digital-divide-health-us` | *Digital divide as a determinant of health in the U.S. older adults: prevalence, trends, and risk factors* | 《美国老年人数字鸿沟作为健康决定因素：现状、趋势与风险因素》
  Method: secondary survey analysis
  Scenario: digital divide and health
  Friction focus: access gap, use gap, cognitive gap
- `older-digital-exclusion-loneliness` | *Digital exclusion and loneliness in older people: panel data analysis of three longitudinal cohort studies* | 《老年人的数字排斥与孤独：三项纵向队列研究的面板数据分析》
  Method: panel data analysis
  Scenario: digital exclusion and wellbeing
  Friction focus: exclusion consequences
- `older-digital-health-era` | *Older adults in the digital health era: insights on the digital health related knowledge, habits and attitudes of the 65 year and older population* | 《数字健康时代的老年人：关于65岁及以上人群数字健康相关知识、习惯与态度的洞见》
  Method: representative survey
  Scenario: digital health
  Friction focus: knowledge, habits, attitudes
- `older-digital-exclusion-cognitive-impairment` | *Digital exclusion and cognitive impairment in older people: findings from five longitudinal studies* | 《老年人的数字排斥与认知受损：来自五项纵向研究的发现》
  Method: longitudinal analysis
  Scenario: digital exclusion and cognition
  Friction focus: exclusion consequences
- `older-internet-use-wellbeing` | *Internet use among older adults: Determinants of usage and impacts on individuals' well-being* | 《老年人的互联网使用：使用决定因素及其对个体福祉的影响》
  Method: secondary survey analysis
  Scenario: general internet use
  Friction focus: determinants and wellbeing
- `older-nuanced-internet-use` | *A nuanced understanding of Internet use and non-use among the elderly* | 《对老年人互联网使用与不使用的细致理解》
  Method: survey comparison
  Scenario: internet users vs non-users
  Friction focus: diversity of non-use
- `older-digital-divide-grown-old` | *The digital divide has grown old: Determinants of a digital divide among seniors* | 《数字鸿沟已“老龄化”：老年群体数字鸿沟的决定因素》
  Method: representative survey
  Scenario: digital divide among seniors
  Friction focus: structural determinants
- `older-social-networking-obstacles` | *Obstacles to social networking website use among older adults* | 《老年人使用社交网站的障碍》
  Method: survey
  Scenario: social networking sites
  Friction focus: social media adoption barriers
